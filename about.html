Who we are

Building shared field intelligence from Northern Virginia.

Located in Northern Virginia and veteran-owned, A² Perceptions sits in the middle of local SAR teams that operate throughout the state and near Washington, D.C., where public-safety experts, emergency managers, federal agencies, and technology leaders converge.

Mission

Help responders make faster, clearer decisions in the field.

A² Perceptions exists to help public-safety teams perceive, share, and act on mission-critical information without pulling attention away from the environment in front of them.

Vision

Make complex field data understandable at the speed of response.

Our vision is a wearable XR mission-awareness platform that turns routes, clues, teammate positions, sensor feeds, and uncertainty into shared spatial context that responders can trust and use.

Our story

From shared-perception research to a SAR-first startup.

A² Perceptions grew out of academic work from Virginia Tech University on shared perception, human-AI teaming, and XR systems for emergency response. Both founders met each other while doing research for their PhD and decided to team up. Their research asked a practical question: how can distributed responders see, share, and coordinate around the same mission context?

That question is now becoming a company. We are refining early research insights into commercial workflows for search and rescue first, with future public-safety applications across police, EMS, disaster response, and field command.
