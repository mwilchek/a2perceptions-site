Shared Field Intelligence for Public Safety Teams

A² Perceptions is developing a wearable XR mission-awareness platform to help search, rescue, police, EMS, and field command teams navigate, coordinate, and share critical context in complex environments.

Why it matters

Responders operate with fragmented information.

Radios, maps, drones, notes, mobile devices, and command updates often live in separate workflows. A² is exploring how wearable XR can bring the most important mission context into the responder’s line of sight.

01

Radio overload

Critical details can be repeated, delayed, or missed when teams are moving fast under stress.

02

Lost context

Location is useful, but responders also need to know what was found, cleared, marked, or updated.

03

Separated tools

Maps, sensor feeds, and mission notes are often split across screens, roles, and devices.

04

Dynamic scenes

SAR, police, EMS, and disaster response teams need shared awareness as the situation changes.

What we are building

A shared operating picture delivered at the edge.

The A² platform concept connects wearable XR, mobile compute, command views, and field data so responders can see spatial cues, share markers, and coordinate without abandoning the environment in front of them.
