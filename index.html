Shared Field Intelligence for Public Safety Teams

A² Perceptions is translating shared-perception research into an early wearable XR platform for search and rescue teams, with future public-safety applications across police, EMS, disaster response, and field command.

Why it matters

Responders make time-critical decisions from fragmented information.

SAR teams often coordinate across radio calls, text messages, witness interviews, map tools, drone feeds, trail cameras, canine teams, and command updates. A² is focused on helping responders see and share the most important context while staying oriented to the mission in front of them.

Platform capabilities

One connected system. Many force multipliers.

A² is focused on a connected field loop that combines wearable XR, real-time data, and team collaboration to deliver the right information to the right person at the right time.

⌖

01 · Navigate

Navigation & Wayfinding

Interactive terrain maps with waypoints, team positions, clue markers, search sectors, and extraction routes keep everyone oriented and moving with purpose.

▣

02 · Capture

Interview & Evidence Capture

Capture witness statements, photos, and key field details. Organize, summarize, and share insights across teams while the information is still fresh.

⌁

03 · Correlate

Multi-Feed Intelligence

Aggregate data from teammate views, drones, trail cameras, and K9 units. Detect patterns, surface high-priority alerts, and push updates to the team.

◎

04 · Coordinate

Team Collaboration

Share a live, interactive 3D map so every team member sees the same picture. Coordinate routes, assign tasks, and adapt as the mission changes.

SAR first

Focused where the need is concrete.
